Hobbies

TRAVELING

I enjoy traveling, hiking, and kayaking, activities that connect me with both nature and people. My global journeys have become more than just personal adventures; they serve as immersive lessons in diversity, respect, and resilience. Each destination offers a new perspective, reminding me of the richness of human experience and the importance of empathy across cultures.

CATS

Spending time with my cat, Tommy, brings daily joy and grounding moments to my life. His playful antics, curiosity, and comforting presence offer a meaningful counterbalance to my academic and professional work. Through the routines of care and companionship, I am reminded of the beauty of simple, present moments and the importance of connection across species.
